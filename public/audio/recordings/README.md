# Engineer Recordings

Place audio files here with the following naming convention:

- `night1.mp3` - Night 1 recording
- `night2.mp3` - Night 2 recording
- `night3.mp3` - Night 3 recording
- `night4.mp3` - Night 4 recording
- `night5.mp3` - Night 5 recording
- `night6.mp3` - Night 6 recording (bad ending monologue)
- `night6_good.mp3` - Night 6 good ending recording (optional)

## Format Requirements

- Format: MP3 (recommended) or OGG
- Sample rate: 44100 Hz
- Bitrate: 128-192 kbps is fine for voice
- Duration: ~60-80 seconds each

## How It Works

The game will automatically play the recording for the current night approximately 30 seconds after the night begins. Players can skip the recording by clicking the "SKIP" button in the bottom right corner.
